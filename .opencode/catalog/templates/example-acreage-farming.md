## What this template is

Premium precision-farming landing: dark/light alternating sections, fullscreen hero video background, animated stats grid, infinite logo marquee, image-backed service cards.

## When to use

Agriculture/precision-farming or industrial B2B with video assets.

## Recommended paired design systems

vercel, stripe, calm -- agriculture/trade, video-led

1. Fullscreen hero video bg -> 2. Dark intro section -> 3. Light stats grid (animated) -> 4. Logo marquee (infinite) -> 5. Service cards (image-backed) -> 6. CTA -> 7. Footer
